AI for Animation & Graphics – In-Person Course (Pitampura)

Unlock your creativity with Pitampura’s best AI animation course. This in-person training helps you master AI design tools like Leonardo.Ai, RunwayML, and Pictory to create professional-level animation and visual content. Affordable and job-focused, this course also covers AI video editing and branding.

Course Modules

Month 1: Foundations of AI in Design

  • Introduction to AI tools (Canva AI, Leonardo.Ai)
  • Prompt writing for creative assets
  • Character & scene generation

Month 2: AI Video & Animation Tools

  • Script-to-video using Pictory
  • Text-to-video animations with RunwayML
  • Voiceovers & avatars using ElevenLabs, HeyGen

Month 3: Real-World Projects

  • YouTube Shorts creation
  • Promo video project
  • Branding kits: logo, banner, carousel

Bonus Module: Blender Integration

  • Preparing AI assets for Blender
  • Import & basic scene setup in Blender
  • Portfolio preparation and career guidance

Who Should Join?

  • Aspiring graphic designers & content creators
  • Students and beginners interested in AI tools
  • YouTubers, freelancers, and influencers
  • Content creators looking to build a portfolio with real AI-driven work
